About Nuclear

Our company is dedicated to making NUCLEAR LEVEL PROGRAMMING GAMES!! We publish a 2d game every weekend and we publish a 3d game every month. In total, per month we will release four 2d games as well as one 3d game. Some games will be free and others will be only accessible to premium members. To get premium, you need to pay $20 a month to access the most NUCLEAR GAMES. Games will be rated on the Nuclear scale from Anomaly to Major Accident, the games that are rated from Anomaly - Serious Incident inclusive will be free while the games rated from Accident with Local Consequences - Major Accident will be premium. The nuclear scale is:


Major Accident (best): 7/7

Serious Accident: 6/7

Accident with Wider Consequences: 5/7

Accident with Local Consequences: 4/7

---------------------------------- Anything lower than this is free ------------------------------

Serious Incident: 3/7

Incident: 2/7

Anomaly (worst): 1/7




This scale is the International Nuclear and Radiological Event Scale which we will use for Nuclear.
